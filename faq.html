Frequently Asked Questions

Are these trails safe for children or beginners?

Most trails listed are Class I, removing the dangers of common city traffic. Because of this, most trails are ideal for families.

What are the general rules for biking in LA?

Always wear a helmet if under 18, yield to pedestrians, and use hand signals.

Does this site own or maintain these trails?

This site manages and updates trail information for beginner and veteran bikers alike. Though we provide information on each trail we cover, we do not own or maintain any LA trail. We do not limit liability for any deaths or personal injuries caused by negligence, fraud, or willful misconduct/gross negligence while exploring any of our highlighted trails.

What essentials should I carry when biking in LA?

Preparation is key for traveling any distance, especially for bikers. Make sure you have some food, water, and a reliable map or GPS for each trip.

Essentials

  • Water: Water is absolutely necessary when biking long distances in LA. Make sure to carry at least two water bottles for every trip or make sure you have water available along the trail.
  • Food: Make sure to prepare some calories on the road. Packing some readiliy available food will help give you the boost of energy you need to finish the trail.
  • GPS: *Always know where you are!* A GPS can help you find your way after getting lost.
  • Helmets: Helmets are non-negotiable for minors (any individual under the age of 18)! Make sure you and your children are following California Safety Laws while exploring LA's biking trails.

    Where can I rent a bike near these trails?

    There are many local bike shops across the Greater Los Angeles Area. Some of the most well-renowned bike shops in the area are listed below:

    • Helen's Cycles (Santa Monica & Multiple Locations) Since 1936, this store has sold bicycles and other biking equipment in the LA area. Make sure to visit to explore their large selection of products and repair services.
    • The Bike Shop Santa Monica (Main St) Specialized for electric bike enthusiasts and those looking for personal, neighborhood-focused service near the beach. If you need a bike on the go, make sure to stop here!
    • Bike Attack (Santa Monica/Playa Vista) Bike Attack is a well-known store for sports products including road, mountain, and electric bikes. If you're looking for an affordable bike, don't skip out on this shop!
    • DTLA Bikes (Broadway) Located in the heart of Downtown Los Angeles, this bike shop is a biker's heaven for urban commuters and cyclists in the area. Recommended for daily biking and varius traveling knickknacks.
    • Beverly Hills Bike Shop Looking to splurge on a fancy new bike? Make your to Beverley Hills Bike Shop for their premium selection of high-quality bicycles and traveling supplies! If you are in the West L.A. are come check out their exquisite selection.
